# Zoning AI Demo Questions

Sample questions that showcase the context caching feature with Milwaukee's entire Zoning Code loaded into Gemini 3's 1M context window.

---

## Basic Zoning Queries

- What uses are allowed in an LB2 (Local Business) district?
- What's the maximum building height in RM4 residential zones?
- Can I open a coffee shop in an RS6 single-family district?
- What are the parking requirements for a restaurant in the downtown area?

---

## Development Feasibility

- I have a 5,000 sq ft lot in RO2 zoning. What's the maximum floor area I can build?
- What setbacks are required for a new construction in RM3?
- Can I build an ADU (accessory dwelling unit) on my property zoned RS5?
- What's the maximum lot coverage allowed in C9A commercial zones?

---

## Use-Specific Questions

- Where can I operate a brewery or taproom in Milwaukee?
- What zoning districts allow mixed-use residential above retail?
- Can I run a daycare center from my home in a residential zone?
- What are the requirements for a food truck commissary?

---

## Overlay & Special Districts

- What additional requirements apply in the Historic Third Ward overlay?
- How does being in a TIF district affect my development options?
- What design standards apply in the Architectural Review Board (ARB) overlay?
- What benefits do Opportunity Zones provide for new development?

---

## Comparison & Analysis

- What's the difference between LB1 and LB2 zoning?
- Compare parking requirements between downtown (DSD) and neighborhood commercial zones
- Which residential zones allow multi-family buildings with 4+ units?
- What's more restrictive for a brewery: IL1 or C9H zoning?

---

## Conditional Use & Variance

- What conditional uses require a public hearing in commercial districts?
- How do I apply for a variance for building height?
- What findings does the Board of Zoning Appeals need to grant a use variance?
- Can I get a conditional use permit for a live/work space?

---

## Real Estate Investment

- I'm looking at a property zoned IO (Industrial-Office). What residential conversion options exist?
- What triggers a zoning compliance review when buying commercial property?
- Can non-conforming uses be expanded under Milwaukee's code?
- What happens to my grandfathered use if the building is destroyed by fire?

---

## Why This Matters

These questions demonstrate how having the entire 12-subchapter Milwaukee Zoning Code cached in Gemini 3's context eliminates hours of manual research. Users get instant, accurate answers that would normally require:

- Hiring a zoning consultant ($150-300/hour)
- Days of research through dense legal documents
- Multiple calls to city planning departments

**Tech:** Gemini 3 with 1M context window + context caching for cost efficiency
